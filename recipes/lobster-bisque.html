Lobster Bisque

Lobster bisque is a creamy french style bisque soup made with lobster meat. This recipe takes about 40 minutes. It incorporates 16 ingredients and serves 2 to 3 people.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb lobster meat, cut up in small pieces
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 2 tbsp minced shallots
  • 2 tbsp chopped green onions
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp Tabasco sauce
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 6 tbsp dry sherry
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 cup hot water
  • tsp lobster base(or bullion, base is better)
  • 4 oz tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 4 tbsp butter

Steps:

  1. Heat some oil on medium high heat and saute shallots, onions, and garlic for one minute.
  2. Deglaze the pan with white wine.
  3. Add the worcestershire, tabasco and thyme and saute for another minute.
  4. Deglaze the pan with the sherry.
  5. Add the paprika, hot water and lobster base and mix well.
  6. Stir in tomato paste and add both bay leaves.
  7. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Whisk in heavy cream, add the butter and bring to a boil.
  9. Add the lobster meat and simmer until opaque/cooked through.
  10. Serve with garlic bread on the side, if available.
